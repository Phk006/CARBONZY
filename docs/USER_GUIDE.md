# User Guide

## Opening The App

Visit:

```text
https://phk006.github.io/CARBONZY/
```

Tap **Get Started** to enter the tracker.

## Tracking Steps

Use **Add 500 Steps** on the Track screen. The app awards 5 coins for each 1,000 steps.

The step card shows:

- Total steps
- Coins earned from steps
- Progress toward 5,000 steps
- Estimated distance
- Estimated calories
- Estimated CO2 saved

## Completing Habits

Tap any habit card once per session to mark it complete and earn coins.

Examples:

- Reusable bottle
- No plastic bag
- Cycled to class
- Lights off
- Plant-based meal
- Screen time under 3h
- Composted waste

Completed habit cards turn green and show a check mark.

## Recycling Flow

Open **Recycle** from the bottom navigation or the Plastic Disposal Tracker card.

Tap the record button. After a few seconds the app shows a disposal preview. Confirm it to earn 25 coins.

If camera permission is unavailable, the app can still show a demo capture.

## Challenges

The Challenges screen shows weekly goals, progress bars, and bonus coin rewards.

Examples:

- Walk 7,000 steps
- Verify 5 disposals
- Log 5 plant meals
- Save energy 3 times
- Earn 500 lifetime coins

## Eco Market

Open **Shop** to browse eco-friendly products. Redeem products using coins.

If you do not have enough coins, the redeem button shows that more coins are needed.

## Profile

The Profile screen shows:

- Lifetime coins
- Score
- Badges
- Purchases

## Demo Limitation

Progress is stored only while the page is open. Refreshing the browser resets the app state.
